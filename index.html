Privacy Policy

Effective 9 July 2026 App GeoRush Platforms iOS & Android

GeoRush is a free, ad-supported hyper-casual arcade game. This policy explains exactly what data the app touches — which is very little, and almost none of it by us directly.

01 Who we are

"GeoRush", "we", "us" and "our" refer to the developer of the GeoRush mobile game. This policy covers the GeoRush apps published on the Apple App Store and Google Play. Questions? See Contact at the end.

02 Information we collect

We designed GeoRush to need as little of your data as possible.

Collected by us — none directly

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email, phone number, or contacts. Your game progress — high score, level, coins, skins, power-up upgrades, daily streak and settings — is stored only on your device (via local storage) and is never sent to us or any server we control.

Collected by our advertising partner — Google AdMob

GeoRush shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve, cap, measure and protect those ads from fraud, Google's SDK may automatically process limited technical data, which can include:

  • a mobile advertising identifier (e.g. Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA/IDFV);
  • coarse device and app information (device model, OS version, language, app version);
  • approximate, IP-derived location (country/region level) and ad-interaction events.

By default GeoRush requests non-personalized ads, which limits this data to what is needed to deliver and measure ads rather than to build an advertising profile.

03 How the information is used

  • To keep the game free — ads fund GeoRush; the data above lets our partner deliver them.
  • To run the game — your on-device progress is read and written locally so your scores and unlocks persist between sessions.
  • To keep ads fair and safe — frequency capping and fraud/abuse prevention.

We do not sell your personal information.

04 Advertising & your choices

Google acts as a third-party advertising vendor. You are in control of ad personalization:

  • iOS: if we ever request tracking, iOS shows an App Tracking Transparency prompt — choosing "Ask App Not to Track" keeps ads non-personalized. You can change this anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Android: reset or delete your advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization, in Settings → Google → Ads.

How Google uses data from apps that use its services is described in Google's own policy: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

05 Sharing & third parties

We do not share personal information with anyone except as needed to show ads through Google AdMob, which processes the data described in section 02 under its own privacy policy. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

06 Children's privacy

GeoRush is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided such information, contact us and we will address it.

07 Data retention & deletion

Because your game data lives only on your device, uninstalling GeoRush deletes it. You can also clear the app's storage from your device settings. Data processed by Google for advertising is retained and deleted according to Google's policies; you can reset your advertising identifier at any time as described in section 04.

08 Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under GDPR or CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or limit the processing of your personal data, and to opt out of personalized advertising. Because we hold no account data about you, most of these rights are exercised directly through your device's ad settings — or by contacting us below.

09 Security

Your progress stays in your device's app storage, protected by your device's own security. Ad requests are transmitted over encrypted connections. No method of storage or transmission is ever completely secure, but we keep our data footprint intentionally small.

10 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves or the law changes. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective" date above and, where appropriate, noted in the app or store listing. Continued use of GeoRush after an update means you accept the revised policy.
