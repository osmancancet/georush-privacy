Frequently asked

How do I play?

Tap anywhere to jump your neon square over obstacles. Tap again in mid-air for a double jump. Grab coins, dodge the flying obstacles, and survive as the world speeds up.

Where is my progress saved?

Your high score, coins, skins and upgrades are stored on your device. They stay as long as the app is installed. Deleting the app clears them.

Why do I see ads?

Ads (via Google AdMob) keep GeoRush free. Banners appear only on the menu and game-over screens — never during a run. You can limit ad personalization from your device's ad settings.

The game won't load / an ad space is blank

Try closing and reopening the app, and check your internet connection. A blank ad space simply means no ad was available — the game keeps working normally.

I found a bug or have a suggestion

Please email us the details — bug reports and ideas genuinely shape the next update.
